Welcome to Sweet Buns Bakery

Cape Town's community bakery – fresh, affordable, made with love.
From artisanal breads to vegan and gluten-free treats, we have something for everyone.

Our Story

Sweet Buns Bakery is a Cape Town-based bakery loved for fresh bread, cakes and pastries. Started in 2017 by Thato and Michael, it has grown into a neighbourhood favourite while staying true to locally sourced ingredients and friendly service.

Why Choose Us?

Unlike large franchises, we focus on local ingredients, friendly service and that “home away from home” feeling. Browse our gallery to see recent creations or enquire about custom orders.
