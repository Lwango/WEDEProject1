Our Story

Sweet Buns Bakery was founded in 2017 by Thato and Michael, two friends passionate about baking. What started as a weekend market stall quickly grew into a neighborhood bakery loved for its fresh bread, cakes, and pastries.

Mission

  • Serve freshly baked goods at fair prices
  • Support local farmers with sustainable ingredients
  • Create a warm, community-driven bakery space
  • Maintain the highest quality and taste standards
  • Blend traditional recipes with modern tastes

Vision

  • Be Cape Town’s most trusted and loved bakery
  • Grow steadily while keeping a family-style atmosphere
  • Encourage healthy eating with fresh, local produce
  • Build an online presence to connect with more people
